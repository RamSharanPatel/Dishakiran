Our Services

We provide app and web-based services for “life design” in the simplest yet most scientific ways to help you understand yourself better and guide you through your journey in life.

Psychometric assessment for children in 8 Indian languages based on their age, to help them understand their true interest, orientation, inclination, personality and aptitude & recommend suitable streams & professions

Psychological counselling to help children cope with the various difficulties they face in a friendly, simple, interesting yet effective way with the aim of getting every child to know that they are worth it

Wellness Suite to assess current state of mind and suggest simple, doable and amazing methods to overcome any kinds of life issues

IQ enhancer to help everyone with the tricks of effective learning and understanding in order to enhance IQ based on credible and proven scientific methods

Mentorship programme for children by giving them appropriate one-on-one guidance and hand-holding thereby enhancing self- belief, self-confidence and a zeal to excel. Conducted by experts who have mentored some of the most successful children

About us

We at Disha Kiran believe that we can help everyone discover their true self by the means of most scientific, proven, established yet very simple methods of assessment. We have a burning desire to reach as many children & adults to design their lives based on their true interests, aptitude, orientation & inclination so that everyone follows their “true north” and enjoys the journey called life

“Imagine Amitabh Bachchan being a cricketer or Sachin Tendulkar being a singer or Dr Abdul Kalam being a politician “– Would they have been as successful?"

Why are some people more successful than others? Is it just hard work or luck or perseverance or is it something much more than that? Disha Kiran believes that it takes doing what you genuinely like, following the path where your inherent interest takes you, being in the domain which synchronizes with your original orientation.

Every child is brilliant and unique and has the potential to rise to great heights, only if the child knows what excites them the most or what is their real passion. Disha Kiran helps every child find their true brilliance and then guides them through the journey of life to reach the stars.

We use psychometric assessment which is the most advanced and uses trusted scientific tools to continuously evolve using artificial intelligence, virtual reality and augmented reality technologies. Come and experience the true power of psychometrics.
